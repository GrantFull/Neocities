Theodore Mason

Theodore Mason was born in the small town of Weilheim in the northern country Sunhold. He is a bookworm with a taste for academia

Body

At one point during his rule, Ronan's capitol was taken by a rouge force. His army was away and the small contingent of royal guards were overrun. Because of the power of his domain, the occupiers could not enter his keep. Ronan however could not leave his keep and his city was occupied for months while his army returned from their travel. In an effort to force him into the open where he was vulnerable the enemies killed 5 people of his kingdom each day at his gates. Eventually his standing army arrived and the enemy retreated. Since then Ronan hasn't been the same.

Esme Mason

Esme is Theodore’s younger sister, the two were exceedingly close as they grew up. When they weren’t learning from their mother or working with their father, they would play in the forests. They often read books borrowed from their nearby church together on the hard floor of their small home. Esme always chose the books of adventurers, tales of gore, glory, and danger. While Theodore tended towards books more grounded in reality like the histories. Theodore’s favorites were poetry books, Esme wouldn’t let him get two in a row because of how long he spent re-reading his favorite passages. As they grew up Esme would always tell Theodore about how she wanted to travel out of their town and see the world. Theodore would try and talk her out of it because it was dangerous (really he didn’t want to leave the safety of Weilheim and Esme leaving would make him lonely and feel like a coward). Eventually when she was old enough, Esme traveled to the nearby capital of Sunhold Cales. After her trip to the capital Esme was decided and set out a month later for good. By this point Theo had been working as a mason.
